About

Surgeon. Scholar. Leader.

Dr. Armin Edalatpour is a plastic surgeon who completed a six-year plastic and reconstructive surgery residency at the University of Wisconsin Hospitals and Clinics and is currently completing an aesthetic surgery fellowship at El Paso Cosmetic Surgery under the direction of Dr. Sadri O. Sozer, M.D., FACS.

His surgical interests span the full breadth of aesthetic and reconstructive surgery, with particular depth in body contouring, facial rejuvenation, breast surgery, and microsurgical reconstruction. He chairs the ASPS Resident & Fellows Forum and serves on multiple national and international society committees.

Prior to medicine, Dr. Edalatpour served as a paramedic, critical care EMT, and program director for emergency medical training — a foundation that informs his commitment to patient safety and procedural excellence.
