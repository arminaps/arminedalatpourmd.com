Focus Areas
✦

Aesthetic Surgery

Body contouring, laser and injectable outcomes, facelift technique, rhinoplasty, and cosmetic practice development — including ASERF-funded studies on TXA and CBD.

✦

Breast Surgery

Autologous free flap reconstruction, prepectoral implant conversion, DIEP flap economics, perfusion imaging, and breast reduction outcomes.

✦

Hand & Upper Extremity

Carpal tunnel release, distal radius fractures, flexor tendon repair, trapeziometacarpal arthritis prevalence, and relative procedural value.

✦

Surgical Education

Residency program analysis, applicant selection modeling, diversity in training pipelines, in-service exam trends, and society-based career development.

✦

Reconstructive Outcomes

Gender-affirming surgery, buried penis reconstruction, headache surgery quality of life, pressure ulcer reconstruction, and microsurgical flap monitoring.

Peer Review & Editorial

Editorial board member, PRS Global Open. Reviewer for PRS, JPRAS, Aesthetic Plastic Surgery, ASJ, Annals of Plastic Surgery, and 15+ additional journals.
